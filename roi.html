Methodology

How We Calculate Your Savings

Our estimates are based on two measurable, real-world impact areas.

Increased Hangar Capacity

Our auto-stacking algorithm is designed to optimally position aircraft to maximize utilization. The revenue estimate uses your hangar size, utilization boost, and monthly tenant rate to project additional annual income.

Labor Cost Reduction

Autonomous tugs and guided tow-path planning are designed to reduce the staff hours needed for stacking. The labor estimate multiplies your staff count, hourly wage, and daily stacking hours across 365 days per year.

Conservative by Design

These figures don't account for incident cost avoidance, insurance benefits from the audit trail, or staff redeployment to higher-value tasks. Real-world impact is typically higher.
