How We Calculate Your Savings

Our ROI calculations are based on real-world performance data

Increased Hangar Capacity

Our auto-stacking algorithm optimally positions aircraft to maximize hangar utilization. By analyzing aircraft dimensions and hangar space in real-time, we can achieve a noticable increase in capacity compared to traditional manual positioning.

Revenue Impact: Each additional aircraft generates money in hangar fees, maintenance services, and related revenue.

Labor Cost Reduction

Autonomous aircraft tugs eliminate the need for manual towing operations, reducing labor requirements. The system operates 24/7 without breaks, shift changes, or overtime costs.

Cost Savings: Based on your labor hours and hourly rate, we calculate annual savings from reduced staffing needs while maintaining full operational capability.

Operational Time Savings

Path planning optimization reduces aircraft movement time greatly. With collision detection preventing delays and autonomous execution eliminating coordination overhead, operations run significantly faster.

Efficiency Gain: Time savings translate to faster turnaround, increased throughput, and improved customer satisfaction.
