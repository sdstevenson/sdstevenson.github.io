How It Works

Upgrade Your Fleet With Confidence

Retrofit your existing tugs with autonomous capabilities without replacing your equipment.

Smart Auto-Braking

STTUGS Collision Prevention seamlessly integrates with your tug's existing braking system, automatically stopping the vehicle if a threat is detected.

Auto-Steer & Path Following

By syncing with STTUGS' tug tow-path planning, your tugs can precisely follow any tow paths, enabling fully autonomous operation.

Redundant Safety

Multiple onboard sensors, dead-mans switches, integration with STTUGS Collision Prevention, and complete user control provide comprehensive safety coverage.

Operator Override

Operators can drive retrofitted tugs manually or let the system guide them. Override switches allow your team to disable auto-steer or auto-braking at any time.
