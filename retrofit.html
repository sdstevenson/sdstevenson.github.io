How It Works

Upgrade Your Fleet With Confidence

Retrofit your existing tugs with autonomous capabilities without replacing your equipment.

Smart Auto-Braking

A compact unit integrates seamlessly with your tug's existing braking system. If the STTUGS Collision Prevention system detects an imminent threat, it automatically stops the vehicle.

Auto-Steer & Path Following

By syncing with STTUGS' generated tow paths, the retrofit upgrades manual equipment with precise, hands-free path following capabilities.

Wireless System Integration

Retrofitted tugs maintain a secure, encrypted connection to your on-site STTUGS server to receive live telemetry, collision alerts, and routing data in real-time.

Operator Override

Operators can drive retrofitted tugs manually or let the system guide them. Override switches allow your team to disable auto-steer or auto-braking at any time.
