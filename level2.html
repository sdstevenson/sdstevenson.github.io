Auto-Stacking & Path Planning

Intelligent algorithms that first determine optimal aircraft positions to maximize hangar capacity, then generate efficient movement paths for users to follow.

Two-Part Optimization Process

First we optimize placement, then we plan aircraft tow paths

1

Auto-Stacking

Our algorithm analyzes aircraft dimensions, hangar geometry, and arrival/departure times to determine the optimal position for each aircraft. The algorithm maximizes capacity while keeping aircraft accessible.

  • Real-time 3D mapping
  • Aircraft dimension analysis
  • Optimal position calculation
2

Path Planning

Once optimal positions are determined, the system generates efficient tow paths. The path maintains a safe distance from other aircraft and provides clear visual guidance on your tablet.

  • Obstacle avoidance algorithms
  • Maintains safe distance during tow
  • Visual path guidance
