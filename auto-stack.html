Key Features

Advanced Capabilities

The intelligence your hangar needs to maximize every square foot.

Optimal Positioning

Smart algorithms analyze aircraft dimensions, hangar geometry, and arrival/departure schedules to determine the most efficient placement for every aircraft.

Dynamic Planning

The layout plan updates in real-time as new aircraft arrive or depart, always keeping the hangar at peak capacity without requiring manual recalculation.

Visual Guidance

Clear, interactive stack plans are displayed on your tablet so operators always know exactly where each aircraft should go — even for complex mixed fleets.

Space Analytics

Continuous monitoring and reporting on hangar utilization, revealing patterns and opportunities to further improve capacity and revenue per square foot.
