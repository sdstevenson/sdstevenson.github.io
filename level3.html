What safety features are built in?

All tugs come with a dead-mans switch and multiple hardware stops and software backups, such as integration with our collision detection system.

Can the tug operate outside on the ramp in the rain/snow, or is it indoor only?

The tug can be operated anywhere, anytime. In the early stages, auto navigation will be limited to inside your hangar.

Does it work with all aircraft types?

Yes. With a unique towbar-less design, our tug can work with any aircraft.

Is remote monitoring and fleet management available for multiple autonomous tugs?

Yes. A cloud-based dashboard will provide real-time telemetry, task scheduling, and health monitoring for fleets operating across sites.

What are the insurance and liability considerations for deploying autonomous tugs?

We will provide safety reports, maintenance logs, and integration documentation to assist customers in discussions with insurers and to support coverage and liability reviews.
