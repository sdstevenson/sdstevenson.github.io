What safety features are built in?

All tugs come with a dead-mans switch and numerous hardware and software stops. Integration with collision detection algorithms further improve safety.

Can the tug operate outside on the ramp in the rain/snow, or is it indoor only?

The tug can be operated anywhere, anytime. In the early stages, auto navigation will be limited to inside your hangar.

How does the tug connect to the nose gear of different aircraft types automatically?

With a unique towbar-less design, our tug can work with a wide range of aircraft: from Cirrus to Gulfstream.
