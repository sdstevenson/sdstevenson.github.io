About Sttugs

Hangar management, reimagined.

Our Mission.

    For decades, FBOs have been limited by an outdated, manual process for hangar stacking. It's slow, prone to costly errors, and leaves profit on the table. We believe that solving this problem is the key to unlocking a new level of FBO efficiency and customer service.

We deliver that key.

    We are pioneering the future of ground operations by combining fully autonomous tugs with complex software to automatically stack aircraft. This creates an error-free hangar management system that completely removes manual labor, maximizes your capacity, and unleashes your true revenue potential.
