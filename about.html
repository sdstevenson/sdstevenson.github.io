Our Mission

STTUGS is on a mission to revolutionize aircraft hangar management through intelligent automation—maximizing safety, efficiency, and profitability for private aviators, FBOs, and MROs worldwide.

Every aircraft that lands depends on the people and processes that keep hangars running. Yet the industry faces compounding pressures such as labor shortages and rising operational costs, with zero margin for error.

Automation is clearly the answer, but current solutions fail to bring about a meaningful change. We believe intelligent hangar automation should work at every scale—from a single T hangar to a full-service FBO facility.

By combining intelligent software with autonomous robotics, STTUGS is building intelligent hangar systems that are not only effective but transformative, enabling operators to run leaner, safer, and smarter.

It's our responsibility to make autonomous operations in aviation accessible—delivering unmatched throughput and safety.
