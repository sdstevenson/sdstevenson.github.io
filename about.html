About Us

Our Mission.

    For decades, FBOs have been limited by an outdated, manual process for hangar stacking. It's slow, prone to costly errors, and leaves profit on the table. We aim to solve this problem using intelligent software and automated aircraft tugs.

Building It Together.

    Solving this problem requires close collaboration with the FBOs who live it every day. We are actively seeking FBO parters to help us develop, test, and deploy a solution that truly works.

Why Partner With Us?

    Influence the product: As an early partner, your feedback directly shapes our development, ensuring a solution to your real-world problems.

    First-mover advantage: Partner FBOs will be the first to deploy our technology and gain a competitive edge in efficiency and service quality.

    Shared success: We're looking for long-term collaborations. Your success is our success.

Meet Our Team.

Where Did the Name Come From?

    The name "Sttugs" is a creative combination of our founder's last name, Stevenson, and "Tugs", the autonomous aircraft tugs that are at the heart of our technology.
