Sttugs is a new kind of aviation software company. We don't just build software — we transform how hangars operate. Our intelligent, autonomous platform prevents collisions, optimizes aircraft positions, and empowers hangar operators to run safer, more efficient operations.

Mission

Sttugs is on a mission to bring autonomous intelligence to every hangar in the world.

We're starting with aircraft hangars, where billions of dollars of assets move daily in tight quarters. Collisions, inefficient layouts, and manual workflows cost the industry hundreds of millions annually. Sttugs changes that — with a platform that sees, thinks, and acts to keep every aircraft safe and every square foot utilized.

Collision Detection & Prevention

Constant sensor-based monitoring of every aircraft and obstacle in the hangar. Real-time alerts and auto-brake triggers prevent accidents before they happen — protecting your assets and your team.

Auto-Stacking Algorithm

Our AI optimizer calculates the ideal layout for your aircraft mix, maximizing every square foot of hangar space. Accommodate more aircraft with fewer moves — at the click of a button.

Path Planning

Generate safe, collision-free tow paths for moving aircraft through congested hangar layouts. Sequence multi-aircraft moves with confidence and eliminate guesswork from ground handling.

Autonomous Tugs

Self-operating tug vehicles equipped with redundant safety systems execute aircraft movements without manual intervention. Redundant obstacle detection and emergency stop systems keep every move safe.

Verifiable Audit Trail

Every aircraft movement, command, and safety event is time-stamped and recorded. Full accountability for compliance, insurance, and operational review — always on, always accurate.

See. Think. Act.

Continuously observing the hangar environment, interpreting aircraft positions with advanced AI, and alerting operators or executing movements autonomously — all in real time. Sttugs learns from every shift, adapting to your fleet mix and delivering safer, smarter operations every day.

Maximize Every Square Foot

AI-powered auto-stacking finds the optimal layout for your specific aircraft mix — then executes it. Move less, fit more, and stop leaving capacity on the table. FBOs report storing up to 35% more aircraft after deployment.

Scale Without Friction

Start with collision prevention and expand to full autonomous tug operations at your own pace. Three scalable tiers ensure you get value from day one — with a clear path to complete hangar automation.

Intelligent Software. Autonomous Hardware.

At Sttugs, we've built a unique software+hardware platform designed specifically for aviation ground handling. Our AI is trained on hangar layouts, aircraft geometries, and real movement patterns — then deployed as an ever-learning, continuously improving system.

Each installation is designed for your operation's specific needs, quickly configured for your aircraft mix and hangar footprint. By uniting perception, planning, and execution in one integrated system, we unlock unprecedented safety and efficiency for any FBO or MRO operation.
