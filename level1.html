How do you ensure accuracy?

Through extensive real-world testing we rigorously test our system for accuracy in real-world conditions.

How are users alerted of potential collisions?

Users will get both a visual and audio queue. Installation of a emergency safety break on your tugs is in development, and will be optionally available.

Are wing-walkers required with this system?

This system eliminates the need for wing walkers, though having one is still encouraged.

Does collision detection work in real time?

Yes, this system works in real time with no delay.

What sensors do you use?

Our system uses a series of cameras to ensure full coverege of your hangar.
